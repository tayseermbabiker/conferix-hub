Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Conferix ("we", "us", "our") operates the conferix.com website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to event alerts or submit an event, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • First name
  • Job title
  • Company name
  • City and industry preferences

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts that collect personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you weekly curated event alerts based on your preferences
  • Improve the relevance of events shown to you
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Conferix:

  • Airtable — to store subscriber and event data
  • Resend — to deliver email alerts
  • Netlify — to host the website and run server functions

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any other parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your subscription is active. If you unsubscribe, your data is marked as inactive. You may request complete deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from email alerts at any time via the unsubscribe link in every email

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely in our third-party service providers' infrastructure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
